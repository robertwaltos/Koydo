                 Curriculum Reform

						 National Steering Committee on Curriculum Reforms

 The role of the National Steering Committee will be to oversee the activities of the Technical Committee and Working Committees by

 Guiding the formulation of relevant policies to facilitate the process of curriculum development, implementation and assessment of the reformed curriculum

 Guiding the development of comprehensive frameworks and strategies for efficient and effective supervision and evaluation of the reformed curriculum for various levels of education and training

 Assuring provision of adequate infrastructure and teaching and learning resources required for the reformed curriculum

 Establishing linkages with partners to ensure adequate funding for the various stages of the curriculum reform process i.e. development, teacher orientation and in-servicing, materials evaluation and provision to schools, monitoring and evaluation of implementation.
