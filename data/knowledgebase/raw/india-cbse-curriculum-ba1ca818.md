BENGALI
(CODE NO OO5)
CLASS- IX, 2024-25
Background

Bengali and its cultural background is an important chapter of Indian history. Bengali language has a
rich tradition of its own. From Charyapada to modern Bengali language the journey is quite interesting.
So one has to know the language to understand the cultural heritage of Bengal. Bengali language
developed since the 10th century and the contribution of many great poets, writers, dramatists,
linguists are to be known sincerely. This very language and its study will play a critical role in inculcating
National and emotional integration in the minds of young students of secondary senior secondary level.
The language curriculum should be created in such a way that it helps to dev elop creativity and
imaginative power among the students. Similarly, it should help them with a powerful communication
skill in both speaking and reading, writing activity.

Objectives

The objectives of teaching Bengali at senior secondary stage are:

1. To know the language which is very rich in its own tradition.
2. To build a sense of confidence in the students in both oral and written form of Bengali language.
3. To know the dialects and sociolect of Bengali language and use it in appropriate social and
regional settings.
4. According to CBSE & NEP, students will be able to prepare themselves to meet 21st century’s
challenges and
develop their capabilities for CBE.
5. To equip the learners with adequate language skill to communicate, to question and in
necessary to raise their
voice against anything immoral.
6. To help them in developing both performance and competence areas of the language and
imparting
information through consulting dictionaries, using internet, getting a book in library on their own.
7. To help them acquiring different registers of Bengali language & integrating various form of
Arts to enrich themselves.
8. To develop a sense of curiosity for extensive reading and to facilitate self-learning, so that the
students
become independent in their learning process.
9. They will also be able to edit and review their own work.
10. So the students will be able to not only communicate in Bengali, they can have other things to
do, as well.
Such as can understand the advertisements, news, weather forecasts, interviews etc
11. They can also narrate pictorial story easily, write formal letters and can individually do official
correspondence.
12. This syllabus will make the students competent in Bengali language in an interesting way.
