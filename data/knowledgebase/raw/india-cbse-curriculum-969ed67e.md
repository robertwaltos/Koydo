BHOTI
SUBJECT (CODE NO. 088)
2024-2025

Background:

         Bhoti script was invented with 4 vowel and 30 consonants derived from fifty
Devnaagri alphabets of India in 7 th Century A.D by Thonmi Sambhota. It is a
complete language having composed 8 books on grammar after the model of
Panini’s Vyakarana of Sanskrit language.
       It is the cultural language of the people of Himalayan region from Ladakh to
Arunachal Pradesh. The works and spirit of today’s much talked Nalanda
University is preserved in Bhoti language. Bhoti language is rich in literature,
which spans from medicine, Astrology,logic, history, philosophy, arts, yoga, drama
and music etc .
Objectives:
1. To build a greater confidence and proficiency in oral and written
communication.
2. To learn and develop understanding in all kinds of traditional knowledge
systems
( Maha-vidyays) like science of language and grammar, traditional medicines,
logic, history, Arts and other allied subjects .
3. To develop ability and knowledge required in order to engage in independent
refection and enquiry.
4. For the preservation and promotion of Bhoti language and literatures.
5. To understand and strengthen Ancient Indian Knowledge systems.
6. To acquaint knowledge on cultural heritage of the region.
7. To develop curiosity creativity through extensive reading.
8. Develop appreciation for Indian languages (Multilingualism).
