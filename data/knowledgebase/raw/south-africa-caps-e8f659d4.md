	  Welcome to the National Department of Basic Education!

	   Welcome to the National Department of Basic Education&rsquo;s website. Here you will find information on, amongst others, the  Curriculum , what to do if you&rsquo;ve  lost your matric certificate , links to  previous Grade 12 exam papers  for revision purposes and our  contact details  should you need to get in touch with us.

   Whether you are a  learner  looking for study guides, a  parent/guardian  wanting a school for your child, a new  teacher  seeking employment or a  researcher  looking for education policies, we&rsquo;ve taken great care to ensure that you can easily access the information on the website. Should you, however, not find what you&rsquo;re looking for please send an email to info@dbe.gov.za and we will gladly assist.
