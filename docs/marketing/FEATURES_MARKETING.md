## Section A: BUILT & FUNCTIONAL — Ready to Market

These features are live, tested, and ready for marketing materials today.

---

### Core Learning Platform

**821+ Learning Modules**
Pre-K through College and beyond. Math, Science, Languages, History, Coding, Medicine, Law, Engineering, and more. Every module includes video lessons, interactive activities, quizzes, and flashcards in a structured progression format.

**Adaptive AI Tutor**
A Socratic-style AI tutor that guides learners through problems without giving direct answers. It adapts to each learner's level, provides personalized explanations, and uses citation-grounded responses to ensure accuracy.

**AI-Powered Placement Assessment**
New learners take a multi-stage diagnostic assessment that adapts in real-time to determine their starting level. No wasted time on content they've already mastered.

**Smart Learning Paths**
AI generates personalized curriculum sessions based on each learner's performance, goals, and knowledge gaps. Weekly study plans adapt as the learner progresses.

**Spaced Repetition & Interleaving**
Scientifically proven review scheduling. KOYDO automatically brings back material right before you'd forget it, mixing practice types for stronger long-term retention.

**Error-Based Remediation Engine**
When a learner gets something wrong, the system doesn't just mark it incorrect — it generates targeted practice worksheets, identifies the specific knowledge gap, and creates a remediation path.

### Interactive Learning Experiences

**100+ Educational Games**
From alphabet airships to quantum quests. Games cover literacy, math, logic, science, creative arts, and more. Each game is curriculum-mapped to specific learning objectives across age ranges 3-18+.

**Science Lab**
Six dedicated science tracks with interactive simulations: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Earth Science, Environmental Science, and Space & Astronomy.

**Speaking Lab**
Real-time pronunciation practice with voice recording and AI-powered feedback. Translation tools and conversation practice for language learners.

Public-domain audiobooks with text-to-speech narration, chapter navigation, and comprehension checkpoints. Read along or listen on the go.

**StoryForge Cinema**
Classic literature transformed into visual learning experiences with embedded comprehension quizzes. Stories become immersive educational journeys.

**Exam Prep Center**
Practice exams for SAT, ACT, CUET, ATAR, A-Level, and more. Full exam simulations with timing, scoring, and detailed domain breakdowns. Error logs feed directly into the remediation engine.

### Gamification & Motivation

**XP & Leveling System**
Every activity earns experience points. Level up, unlock achievements, and track progress with satisfying visual celebrations.

**Daily & Weekly Streaks**
Build learning habits with streak tracking. A visual streak calendar shows your consistency, and streak freezes earned at milestones protect your progress.

**Achievement Badges**
Earn badges for milestones — first lesson, 7-day streaks, perfect scores, speed runs, and more. Share achievements with friends and family.

**5 Mascot Companions**
Choose your learning buddy: Pixel (tech), Echo (music), Spark (science), Terra (nature), or Luna (space). Your companion grows with you through your learning journey.

**Experience Hub**
A spatial experience center with galaxy maps, trophy rooms, and a visual representation of your entire learning journey. See how far you've come.

**Reward Realm**
Unlock fun bonus games by meeting educational milestones. Learn to earn play — a built-in motivation engine that rewards consistent effort.

### Family & Safety

**Parent Dashboard**
Complete visibility into your child's learning. See time spent, subjects covered, scores, streaks, and AI usage. Available for every learner profile in your family.

**Multi-Learner Profiles**
One account, multiple learners. Create profiles for each child in your household with age-appropriate content gating and individual progress tracking.

**COPPA Compliance**
Built from the ground up for children's privacy. No ads, no tracking for children under 13, verified parental consent required, and full compliance with COPPA, FERPA, and GDPR.

**Parental Controls**
Restrict subjects, manage screen time, control AI access, and monitor all activity. Parents stay informed with weekly digest emails and milestone notifications.

**Airplane Mode**
Download lesson packs for offline learning. Perfect for travel, limited connectivity, or screen-time management with pre-approved content.

**Content Moderation**
AI-powered content safety with closed fail-mode for children. If the moderation system can't verify content is safe, it blocks by default.

### Secret Roadmap Items

**1. AI Course Builder for Educators** (Phase 2-3)
AI-powered tools that let teachers build complete custom modules and quizzes from scratch. Upload a textbook chapter → get a full interactive module with video scripts, quiz questions, flashcard sets, and assessment rubrics. This is a major B2B selling point — don't telegraph it.

**2. Blockchain-Verified Credentials** (Phase 3)
Tamper-proof, employer-verifiable certificates secured on blockchain. Direct LinkedIn integration for instant portfolio updates. Credly partnership under evaluation. First-mover advantage in K-12 blockchain credentials.

**3. Voyager Zero — Spatial Computing / VR/AR** (Phase 3)
Full VR/AR immersive learning environments. Science labs you can walk through, historical events you can witness, math concepts you can manipulate in 3D space. Device gateway already hardened (E-17). Keep this completely under wraps until hardware partnerships are finalized.

**4. Creator Marketplace** (Phase 3-4)
Community-contributed modules with revenue sharing. Educators worldwide can create, publish, and monetize their content on KOYDO. Think "Teachers Pay Teachers" but with AI-assisted creation and built-in distribution to millions of learners. Network effects make this a moat.

**5. Predictive At-Risk Detection** (Phase 3)
ML pipeline analyzing learning patterns to predict which students are falling behind BEFORE they fail. Proactive intervention alerts to parents and teachers. Uses xAPI event streams already being collected. Enterprise selling point — don't share methodology.

**6. StoryForge Full Pipeline** (Phase 2-3)
Book-to-film pipeline processing 75,000+ public domain books into cinematic learning experiences with AI-generated narration, visual stills, music scoring, and embedded comprehension. The scale of this content library is the competitive moat — don't reveal the automation pipeline.

**7. Project Atlas — In-House Billing Platform** (Phase 3-4)
RevenueCat-compatible in-house billing platform eliminating third-party dependency. Full subscription management, family billing, institutional licensing, and multi-currency support. Reduces per-transaction costs by 60%+ at scale.

**8. Adaptive Multi-Modal Content Generation** (Phase 3)
AI generates personalized lesson content on-the-fly based on individual learner needs. Not just recommending existing content — creating new explanations, practice problems, and visual aids in real-time. This is the "infinite personalized textbook" vision.
