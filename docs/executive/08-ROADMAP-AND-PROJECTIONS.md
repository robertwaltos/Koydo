## Executive Summary

Koydo has completed all 17 product epics and 36 launch workstreams with a 100% completion rate. The 12-week sprint plan achieved 10/10 green quality gates. The platform is positioned for a multi-surface launch (Web → Google Play → iOS) with a target date of Q1 2026. Strategic growth vectors include curriculum expansion to 1,000+ modules, institutional (B2B) licensing, StoryForge Cinema content, internationalization to 10 languages, and a gamification experience designed to compete with premium entertainment apps.
